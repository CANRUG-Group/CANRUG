This is the official website of the Canadian Regional User Group (CANRUG) for libraries in Canada that use Ex Libris library products.

About CANRUG

CANRUG is the Ex Libris User Group of North America (ELUNA) regional user group for Canada. It serves as a forum for sharing information and expertise in the use of Ex Libris library products in Canada.
